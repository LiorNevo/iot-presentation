IoT and Me
As you can imagine, IoT communicates over many technologies. Ones you might be familiar with are Bluetooth and Wi-Fi ZigBee and Z-Wave are also technologies that are prevalent with IoT because of low power usage and low latency. The downside to using ZigBee and Z-Wave is that you would need an external hub to bridge the communication and consume it.
